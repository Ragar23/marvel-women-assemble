HOW TO PLAY?

Your mission is to prevent the space dogs from getting to you. For each space dog you kill, you get 10 points

KEYBOARD INSTRUCTIONS

  • ArrowUp --> moves up
  • ArrowDown --> moves down
  • ArrowLeft --> moves forward
  • ArrowRight --> moves backwards
  • Press S --> shoots
